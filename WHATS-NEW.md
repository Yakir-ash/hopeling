# WildHope - What's New (July 4, 2026)

One day of updates took the app from v22 to v33. Here's everything, in plain language.

## Your Grove - a living companion
The heart of the new Home screen. A tree that grows through 8 stages (sleeping seed to ancient grove) as your daily streak grows. Animal friends - a robin, a bee, a butterfly, a squirrel, a hedgehog, a fox, an owl, a deer - arrive at streak milestones and **never leave**, even if your streak breaks. Miss a day and your grove rests; it never dies. One action wakes it up.

**Tree rings:** past streaks aren't lost anymore. Every streak of 3+ days becomes a ring in your grove's story ("5-day streak, Jun 2026"), including streaks from before this update.

## A calmer, more beautiful Home
- The daily fact card now shows a real wildlife photo matched to the fact, with the animal's name - tap it to explore that species.
- The screen opens with a clear story: your grove, the current event, one fact, one action. Everything else moved below.
- The daily challenge and weekly missions merged into one Missions card, and duplicated sections were removed.

## Plastic-Free July - the first seasonal event
A live event banner with three missions for July (refuse single-use plastic 5 times, do 2 cleanups, take any 8 actions). Completing them all earns a limited badge that only this event could give. Future events arrive automatically, no app update needed.

## Celebrations that feel like something
Completing an action now bursts leaves and sparkles across the screen with a little vibration. Badges are tappable and explain exactly how to earn them, with progress bars.

## Explain simply
A toggle (in Settings, and right on lessons) that rewrites every lesson and species science blurb in kid-friendly language. Great for reading with children.

## More of the wild world
Four new categories - bats, frogs and amphibians, butterflies and moths, wetlands - each with species photos, threats, hope stories, and actions. That's 26 categories, 39 actions, and 7 courses now.

## Good news, on autopilot
A bot now scans reputable outlets (BBC, The Guardian, Mongabay, and 20+ more) every Monday for positive conservation headlines and adds the best ones to the Good News feed. Strictly filtered: trusted sources only, uplifting stories only. The app updates itself - no new version needed.

## Under the hood
- Fixed: a flash of the home screen when opening badge details (and every other panel).
- Fixed: some daily photos silently failing to load.
- Closing a panel now returns you exactly where you were.
- Every release is verified by an automated 88-check test suite before it ships.

*WildHope - small actions, real hope.* 🌿
