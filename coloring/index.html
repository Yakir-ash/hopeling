🖍 The coloring & games shelf

Real wildlife coloring pages drawn by artists at America’s public wildlife agencies, plus printable nature puzzles made by Hopeling - mazes that bring an animal home, word searches, and criss-cross puzzles where every clue is a true thing about a real animal. All of it prints crisp and ink-friendly on A4 or Letter. Grown-ups: everything here is free and there is nothing to buy.
