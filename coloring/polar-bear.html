Polar bear

🐻‍❄️ lives among The Arctic
Did you know? A polar bear's fur is not really white. Each hair is see-through and hollow, like a tiny straw, and it bounces the light around so the bear looks white. Under all that fur its skin is black, to soak up the sun. And it can smell a seal hiding under a whole metre of snow!
True thing: Polar bear fur is transparent and hollow, not white, and the skin underneath is black. (Polar Bears International)
How it is doing: this animal needs us to be careful.
