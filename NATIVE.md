# Hopeling Native - The Definitive Version
### The PWA is the blueprint, the content source, and the behavioral oracle. The website is the public institution. This app is the companion: the most forgiving, most alive, most trustworthy habit companion for the living world. Not a port. A surpassing.

---

## 1. The vision in one paragraph

The PWA proved the loop: one fact, one act, a tree that forgives. The native app's job is to make that loop FELT. The phone can whisper back (haptics), notice you gently (notifications that never nag), live on your home screen (widgets), work in your pocket on a mountain (offline-first), and render a tree that is genuinely alive at 120fps. The measure of success: a user who misses a week opens Hopeling anyway, because it is the one app on their phone guaranteed to be kind to them. **Hopeling should forgive people better than any app ever built.** That is the brand at native fidelity.

## 2. The three principles that separate the app from its siblings

1. **The website informs the world; the app accompanies one person.** Nothing communal-anonymous lives here beyond the rain; nothing personal lives on the website.
2. **The phone whispers, never shouts.** Every native capability is admitted only if it can be used quietly: haptics under the thumb, one daily notification maximum by default, widgets that show rather than demand.
3. **The PWA rules are law.** dailyIndex, streak forgiveness, rings, freezes, repair, grove stages, backup schema: identical results, verified by tests ported from scripts/sim.js. Native changes the body, never the soul.

## 3. Feature-by-feature: what each system becomes

**The Tree** - unchanged: stages, thresholds, meaning, calm dignity (never a nagging pet). Redesigned: from emoji to a hand-authored Rive character (the EXPERIENCE.md bet, now buildable) - branches that extend at milestones, leaves that unfold rather than appear, seasons from the real calendar (hemisphere-aware), wind that strengthens toward evening, sway following device tilt (gyro, degrade to timer), rain that beads on leaves when the world acts. Touch a leaf: it dips, springs back, faint tick. New moment: growth happens ON SCREEN with a slow warm haptic crescendo, replayable from the journey. Offline: fully local. OS: the tree's current stage is the widget and the themed app icon. Tests: stage thresholds identical to sim; golden tests per stage/season; reduced-motion renders a still, beautiful tree.

**Daily action** - unchanged: one action, honest why, real sources, two minutes. Redesigned: completion is a HOLD, not a tap - the Thumb Promise (see 5). Native: home-screen widget with the action and hold-to-complete; notification actions Done / Tonight / Why; offline completion queues and reconciles; optional private photo memory (on-device only, biometric-lockable, never required, never uploaded). New moment: the completion ceremony distinguishes effort (outdoor) from consistency (streak day) with different 3-second closings. Tests: queued completions survive kill/reboot; clock-change and timezone suites.

**Streaks and forgiveness** - unchanged: every rule. Redesigned presentation: absence is never red, never broken-glass; return after N days opens with the fox moment ("waited 6 days. It never left.") before any numbers. Repair-yesterday becomes a physical gesture: pull yesterday's fallen leaf gently back onto the branch. Native: streak in the widget as tree health, not a number with a bomb attached; timezone-safe (compute in local civil date, reconcile with server time to survive clock cheating without accusing anyone). Tests: DST transitions, reinstall restore, multi-device same-day conflict (max wins), backwards clock.

**Explore / species / Learn** - unchanged: content contract, sources, child + adult text layers. Redesigned: worlds become full-bleed atmospheres entered by a dive transition; species are swipeable portrait cards (photo, call audio where credible - Xeno-canto with credits, status bar drawing itself); lessons read like small books with page-turn physics and read-aloud (system TTS - doubles as accessibility and kids mode). Native: offline packs per world (photos + text pre-downloaded, explicit storage control); deep links (hopeling://species/vaquita) shared from the website open here; save-for-later collections. Deliberately NOT: camera species-ID (credibility risk), AR (parked until it can be excellent). Tests: pack download interruption, deep-link routing matrix, TTS a11y.

**Guardians** - unchanged: the species, the stories, the meaning of the vow. Redesigned: pledging is the sacred gesture - hold on the portrait, the gold ring draws under your finger with a rising haptic pattern, completing with the app's single heaviest haptic + two seconds of silence. Native: an annual anniversary notification written as a letter; guardian news arrives as letters, not alerts ("A letter about your kakapo"); a guardian widget; a shareable card rendered natively to the gallery. New moment: open the app on your pledge anniversary and the grove is already celebrating. Tests: pledge state in backup schema unchanged; letter scheduling; card golden test.

**Circles / rain / pulse** - unchanged: small private circles, invite codes, the flame, cheers, Quiet Grove, the REAL counter (never invented activity - constitutional). Redesigned: presence becomes ambient - a circle-mate's action falls as a named drop with a haptic softer than your own (you learn to feel the difference between "I acted" and "someone I love acted" - no other app has this). Native: notification grouping into one quiet daily summary; deep-link invites (tap a WhatsApp link, land inside the circle); shared family-grove widget; Live Activity only during rare global events (Migration Week). Tests: pulse reconciliation after offline days; cheer idempotency; notification-grouping behavior.

**Ceremonies** - a formal hierarchy replacing confetti-for-everything. Small (daily completion): leaf burst + light tick, under 1.5s. Medium (badge, level, week): medallion stamp + medium impact, 2.5s. Sacred (planting, pledge, repair, return, anniversary, course-binding): full-screen, sound optional, silence used deliberately, gold used exclusively here, 4 to 6s, skippable always. Rare enough to stay sacred; the ceremony index is a design artifact, not a code accident.

Long-term tier: 25. **The Rive Tree itself** - the character (the single biggest investment). 26. **Reusable-Bag Geofence** - opt-in, on-device geofence at your chosen store: "🛍 the bag." ## 5. The languages

**Interaction:** tap = look, hold = promise, swipe = travel, pull-down = roots/refresh, pinch = closer to the living thing. Nothing destructive is ever a single tap.
**Haptic (7 words total):** tick (selection, 25/50/75% of holds) · settle (card landing) · your-drop (warm double) · their-drop (single soft) · commit (medium, promise completed) · bloom (heavy, sacred only) · relief (repair complete). Never haptic on scroll, never on errors (errors get words, not slaps).
**Motion:** RISE / SWAY / FALL / BLOOM from WEBSITE-VISION.md, at native fidelity: 300ms rise with 1deg settle, sway 3 to 7s desynced, only rain crosses the screen, bloom reserved. Every animation has a reduced-motion twin (opacity only) and a low-power twin (none).
**Notifications:** one per day maximum by default, written in the robin's voice, always actionable, always quiet hours 21:00 to 08:00 local, Silent Sunday available, guardian letters monthly, circle summaries daily-digest. Zero marketing notifications, forever. The philosophy in one line: a notification is the robin tapping the window, not a salesman knocking.
**Widgets:** Pocket Seed (action), Tree (stage + weather), Family Grove (flame). All render from local state, update via background refresh, and never show stale streak numbers (compute-on-render).

## 6. Offline and sync

Local-first always: the save is a single JSON document in Contract-1 shape, persisted locally (SharedPreferences now, Isar when relations arrive), synced whole to Supabase saves.data - which means PWA restore, native backup, and multi-device are one code path. Completions queue offline with local timestamps; reconciliation merges by day (max actions wins, never lost). Content caches whole (version-gated); packs pre-download media. Auth failure never blocks use: the app is a guest by default, an account only adds cloud and circles. Reinstall: sign in, Migration ceremony, everything returns. Tests: interrupted sync, airplane week, clock skew, DST, conflicting devices.

## 7. Accessibility (from birth, not retrofit)

Semantics labels on every meaningful animation ("your tree, a young sapling, swaying gently"); Dynamic Type respected to 200% with layouts that reflow rather than truncate; all meaning color-independent (the IUCN bar carries text); reduced motion = still beauty; every haptic has a visual twin; TTS read-aloud on all long text; touch targets 48dp; one-hand reachability; Hebrew and RTL readiness in the layout system from slice one (his family is the first test market); kids-layer text (simpleText) is also the low-literacy mode.

## 8. Architecture (deliberately boring, deliberately durable)

- **lib/core/**: theme (Dawnlight tokens), motion, haptics, clock (the ONE place dates are computed).
- **lib/data/**: content (contract models + cache), save (Contract-1 document + local persistence + sync engine), api (Supabase REST - the same hand-rolled surface as social.js, no SDK lock-in).
- **lib/features/**: grove, today, explore, learn, guardians, circles, ceremonies - each a folder with screen + logic + tests.
- **State:** plain ChangeNotifier per feature now; Riverpod only when cross-feature graphs demand it (do not pre-import a framework the app has not earned).
- **Storage:** SharedPreferences (document) → Isar when species packs/journal arrive.
- **Native bridges** behind tiny interfaces (Haptics, Notify, Widgets) so tests run pure Dart.
- **Testing:** sim.js rules ported into test/rules/ as the oracle; golden tests for ceremonies and tree stages; integration tests on a real low-end Android profile.
- **Flags:** a simple local feature-flag map so slices can ship dark.
- **Privacy analytics:** none beyond GoatCounter-style aggregate opens, or nothing at all. Crash reporting only with explicit consent.

## 9. Performance budgets

Cold start under 2s on a low-end device; first frame interactive under 1s warm; tree at 60fps minimum (120 where hardware allows), particles capped at 24, all imagery cached with width-appropriate variants, widget updates at most 4x/day on battery, background refresh piggybacks on content Mon+Thu cadence. Quality tiers: Full / Calm (no particles, no tilt) / Still (low-power), auto-detected, user-overridable.

## 10. The fifteen slices (each compiles, runs on your phone, tested, never leaves the app broken)

1. **Foundation + the Thumb Promise** (today): design tokens, real icon, paper splash, Contract-1 state document, haptic + motion language, hold-to-complete with progressive ticks, reduced-motion support, rules tests. Risk: none - it ships in this commit.
2. **Content offline** (S): full contract models, cache, version gating; Explore v1 (worlds list + world page). Done when airplane mode shows yesterday's content perfectly.
3. **Sign-in + the Migration** (M): OTP flow, cloud restore as ceremony, guest mode preserved. Done when your real PWA tree appears on your phone. THE de-risking slice - moved early on purpose.
4. **The full forgiveness engine** (M): freezes, repair gesture, rings, welcome-back fog; sim-ported rule tests green. Done when every sim streak case passes identically.
5. **Species + portraits** (M): swipeable species, photos, IUCN draw, save-for-later. 6. **Learn** (M): courses as books, quizzes, read-aloud. 7. **Notifications v1** (S): the Robin, actions, quiet hours, Silent Sunday. 8. **Rain + pulse live** (S): your drop joins the real counter, Drop Distinction haptics, offline queue with the honesty leaf. 9. **Guardians + the gold ring** (M): pledge gesture, letters, anniversary, wallet card. 10. **The Pocket Seed widget** (M): Android first. 11. **Circles** (L): create/join/board/flame/cheers, deep-link invites, digest notifications. 12. **Ceremony pass** (S): the full hierarchy, replayable journey. 13. **The Rive tree** (L): the character - commissioned asset animated by code (the bet; everything above makes it twice as valuable). 14. **Kids + accessibility deep pass** (M): simple mode, TTS everywhere, Dynamic Type audit, Hebrew/RTL. 15. **Release hardening** (M): performance tiers, background sync soak tests, Play internal testing.

Order rationale vs the old plan: cloud restore moved from slice 2 to 3 (behind content, because the Migration ceremony needs the tree to regrow through real stages), notifications before widgets (widgets depend on the notification/background plumbing), circles late (largest surface, needs deep links + notifications mature), the Rive tree deliberately after guardians and ceremonies so the character arrives into a world already worthy of it.

## 11. Deliberately not building

Public feeds, follower counts, leaderboards beyond circles, streaks-as-pressure mechanics, camera species-ID, AR before it can be excellent, health-app integration, microphone anything, precise location anything, ads and analytics SDKs, chat (circles stay presence, not messaging - moderation debt kills small apps), and any notification that exists for retention rather than for the user.

## 12. The three "I have never seen an app do this" experiences

1. **The world rains on you in real time, and you can feel whose drop it is.** Real counter, real people, three haptic voices. Unfakeable, unprecedented.
2. **The Thumb Promise.** An entire app where commitment is physical: every promise in Hopeling is made by holding, with the phone gently confirming under your finger. One gesture philosophy, zero other apps have it.
3. **The Migration.** Sign in on a new phone and watch your years replay: rings counting up, tree regrowing, badges landing. Your history arrives as a ceremony instead of a spinner.

The website keeps the world's record. This app keeps one person's promise. Slice 1 ships now.
