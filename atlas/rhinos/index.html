The picture

Rhinos have roamed for millions of years, but demand for their horn - made of keratin, like fingernails - fuels intense poaching. Intensive protection has stabilised some populations.

The science

Family Rhinocerotidae. Rhino horn is keratin and has no proven medicinal value.
