The picture

Whales are ecosystem engineers. Their nutrient-rich waste fertilises tiny phytoplankton that produce oxygen and capture carbon - a process sometimes called the “whale pump.” After the global whaling ban, several populations are recovering, one of conservation’s clearest wins.

The science

Order Cetacea. A blue whale is the largest animal ever known to have lived - larger than any dinosaur.
