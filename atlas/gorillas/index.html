Gorillas

The picture

Mountain gorillas are one of conservation’s great comeback stories: careful protection and community-based tourism lifted them above 1,000 individuals. They share about 98% of their DNA with humans.

The science

Genus Gorilla. They live in close family groups led by a dominant “silverback” male.
