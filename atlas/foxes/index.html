Foxes

The picture

Foxes are adaptable and mostly stable, thriving even in cities. Their biggest hidden threat is secondary poisoning from rat poisons that move up the food chain.

The science

Small actions that reach them

Go pesticide-free in your garden
Pesticides harm pollinators and cause secondary poisoning up the food chain.
~20 min
Teach a child one animal fact
Early nature connection is the strongest predictor of lifelong conservation behavior.
~10 min
Drive slower on rural roads at dusk
Millions of wild animals die in vehicle collisions yearly; dawn and dusk are peak crossing times.
~5 min
Make a gap for wildlife in your fence
Hedgehogs, frogs and small mammals need to roam between gardens to find food and mates.
