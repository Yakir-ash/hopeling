Orangutans

Palm-oil-driven deforestation is the primary threat to their forests.

← The Atlas

The picture

Orangutans are the only great apes in Asia and spend most of their lives in the trees. All three species are Critically Endangered, mainly because their rainforest is cleared for palm oil and timber.

The science

Genus Pongo. Orangutans use tools and pass learned skills between generations - a form of culture.

In numbers

3Great ape species in Asia

Worth repeating at dinner

Orangutans build a fresh sleeping nest in the trees almost every night.

- WWF

What is already working

Certified palm oil

Rehabilitation & release

Rescued orangutans are rehabilitated and returned to protected forest.
