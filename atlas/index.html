The Atlas

Most encyclopedias tell you what is dying. This one also tells you what is working, and hands you one thing to do before dinner.

🐢Sea Turtles

6 of 7 sea turtle species are threatened; plastic and bycatch are top threats.

Endangered🐋Whales

Many great whales are recovering after the 1986 whaling moratorium.

Vulnerable🦈Sharks

About a third of sharks and rays face extinction, largely from overfishing.

Vulnerable🐬Dolphins

Most dolphins are stable, but river dolphins and the vaquita are critical.

Least Concern🐘Elephants

Keystone species that disperse seeds of over 100 tree species.

Endangered🦍Gorillas

Mountain gorillas rose above 1,000 - community tourism funded the recovery.

Critically Endangered🦧Orangutans

Palm-oil-driven deforestation is the primary threat to their forests.

Critically Endangered🐅Tigers

Wild tigers are rising in India, Nepal and Bhutan.

Endangered🦁Lions

Africa has lost most of its lions in a century, but guarded reserves and herder partnerships are turning it around.

Vulnerable🐼Pandas

Downlisted from Endangered to Vulnerable in 2016 - a real success.

Vulnerable🦏Rhinos

Three of five rhino species are Critically Endangered; poaching is the driver.

Critically Endangered🐺Wolves

Apex predators whose return reshapes whole ecosystems.

Least Concern🦊Foxes

Mostly stable; urban coexistence and rodenticide reduction help most.

Least Concern🐧Penguins

Many penguin species decline as fish stocks and sea ice shift.

Endangered🐕Dogs

Adoption and spay/neuter directly reduce shelter euthanasia.

Least Concern🐈Cats

TNR humanely stabilizes community cat populations; indoor cats protect birds.

Least Concern🦜Birds

North America lost ~3 billion birds since 1970; windows and cats are key threats.

Near Threatened🐝Bees & Pollinators

Pollinators support ~75% of leading food crops.

Vulnerable🌊Oceans

Oceans absorb ~25% of CO₂; overfishing and plastic are top stressors.

🐟Coral Reefs

Reefs support ~25% of marine species; heat waves cause bleaching.

Critically Endangered🌳Forests

Forests host ~80% of land biodiversity; deforestation is slowing in places.

💧Freshwater

Freshwater species populations fell ~83% since 1970.

🐄Farm Animals

Reducing meat and choosing higher-welfare products cut the most suffering.

🦇Bats

Vital night pollinators and pest-eaters; white-nose syndrome and habitat loss are the big threats.

Vulnerable🐸Frogs & Amphibians

The most threatened group of vertebrates on Earth; a deadly fungus and habitat loss drive the crisis.

Endangered🦋Butterflies & Moths

Beautiful pollinators in steep decline; native plants and pesticide-free gardens bring them back fast.

Vulnerable🌾Wetlands

Earth’s most valuable ecosystem per acre - and among the fastest disappearing.

🐻‍❄️The Arctic

Polar bears hunt seals from sea ice, so their future is tied to how much ice the Arctic keeps.
