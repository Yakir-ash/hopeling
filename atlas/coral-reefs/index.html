Coral Reefs

Reefs support ~25% of marine species; heat waves cause bleaching.

← The Atlas
Least ConcernCritically EndangeredExtinct

The picture

Coral reefs cover less than 1% of the ocean floor yet support around a quarter of all marine species. Marine heatwaves cause corals to bleach, but reducing local stressors makes reefs more resilient.

The science

Corals are animals that live in partnership with algae (zooxanthellae) which give them colour and food; heat stress breaks that partnership.

Have one meatless meal
