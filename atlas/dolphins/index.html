Dolphins

The picture

Most ocean dolphins are doing relatively well, but several river dolphins and the vaquita porpoise are among the most endangered mammals on Earth. Dolphins are highly social and use signature whistles like names.

The science

Family Delphinidae. Dolphins use echolocation - bouncing sound off objects - to hunt and navigate.
