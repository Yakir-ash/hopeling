🐬 One of the rarest lives on Earth

Vaquita

Phocoena sinus · ~10 left on Earth

← All guardians

The world's smallest porpoise lives in one small corner of Mexico's Gulf of California - and nowhere else. Gillnets set for other fish nearly erased them. Net bans and round-the-clock patrols are their lifeline now, and every single one still swimming matters.

For young readers

The vaquita is the world's smallest porpoise, a shy little sea cousin of dolphins. Only a handful are left, in one corner of the sea in Mexico, and they need people to fish there more carefully.
